# The YHC Way - New User Setup Guide

Welcome to The YHC Way! This guide will walk you through setting up your account and connecting your tools.

---

## Step 1: Create Your Account

1. Visit the app and click **Get Started**
2. Fill in your details:
   - Email address (use your work email)
   - Password (at least 8 characters)
   - First and last name
3. Click **Create Account**
4. Wait for admin approval (you'll be notified when approved)

---

## Step 2: Log In

1. Go to the login page
2. Enter your email and password
3. Click **Sign In**
4. You'll see the Dashboard - your home base

---

## Step 3: Connect Your Services

Go to **Connect App** in the sidebar to link your tools. The more you connect, the more powerful the app becomes.

### Gmail (Required)
1. Click **Connect** next to Gmail
2. Sign in with your Google account
3. Allow access to read your emails
4. Your emails will appear in the Mailbox

### Google Calendar
1. Click **Connect** next to Google Calendar
2. Sign in with the same Google account
3. Your calendar events will appear in the Calendar view

### Slack
1. Click **Connect** next to Slack
2. Authorize the app to access your workspace
3. Your messages and DMs will sync to the Mailbox

### Zoom
Zoom is set up by your admin. Your meetings will automatically appear in Calendar.

### Apple Calendar (Optional)
If you use iCloud Calendar:
1. Click **Connect** next to Apple Calendar
2. Enter your Apple ID email
3. Create an app-specific password at [appleid.apple.com](https://appleid.apple.com):
   - Go to Sign-In and Security
   - Click App-Specific Passwords
   - Generate a new password named "The YHC Way"
4. Enter the password and click Connect

---

## Step 4: Explore the Main Features

### Overview (Dashboard)
Your unified activity feed showing recent emails, messages, and meetings from all connected services.

### Mailbox
All your emails and Slack messages in one place. Click any item to read, reply, or take action.

### Calendar
See all your events from Google Calendar, Apple Calendar, and Zoom meetings together.

### Projects
Manage tasks with Kanban boards:
- Create projects and add tasks
- Drag tasks between columns
- Set due dates and priorities
- Add dependencies between tasks

### Daily Hub
Check in with your team:
- See pinned announcements
- Share daily updates
- Stay aligned on priorities

---

## Step 5: Take the Guided Tour

When you first log in, a guided tour will highlight key features. You can restart the tour anytime from Settings.

---

## Quick Tips

- **Search**: Use the search bar at the top to find emails, tasks, or messages
- **Keyboard shortcuts**: Press `Cmd+K` (Mac) or `Ctrl+K` (Windows) to open quick actions
- **Notifications**: Enable browser notifications to stay updated
- **Profile**: Click your avatar in the top-right to access settings
