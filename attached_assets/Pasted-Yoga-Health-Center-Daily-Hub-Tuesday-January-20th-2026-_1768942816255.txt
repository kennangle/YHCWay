How to sell a gift card:
-Go to clients profile
-Point of sale
- to the far right you will see “Payments/giftcards”
-If you scroll down you will see “giftcards(custom amount)”
-Put your cursor in the box that say “Gift Card ID”
-Scan the barcode on the back of the empty giftcard
-Type the amount you want on the gift card into BOTH the price and value box.
-Add the item and proceed with regular checkout routine (save print no receipt, CC)

How to use a giftcard:
-Go to clients profile
-Go to point of sale
-Add item(s) that the student wants to purchase
-Click the box that says “Giftcard Balance-Lookup” just to check amount 
-Scan the Barcode on the back of the card
-Proceed with regular checkout routine form of payment would be :prepaid gift
