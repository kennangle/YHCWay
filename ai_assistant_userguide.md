# AI Assistant User Guide

The AI Assistant is your intelligent companion in The YHC Way, designed to help you work smarter, save time, and stay on top of your responsibilities.

---

## Quick Start

1. **Open the Assistant**: Click the brain icon in the bottom-right corner of your screen
2. **Choose a Feature**: Select from the tabs at the top of the panel
3. **Get Results**: The AI processes your request and provides actionable output
4. **Copy or Act**: Use the copy button or take action directly from the results

---

## Daily Briefing

Start each day with a personalized overview of what needs your attention.

### What You'll See

- **Priority Tasks**: Tasks due today or overdue
- **Upcoming Meetings**: Your calendar for the day with attendee info
- **Urgent Messages**: Emails and Slack messages flagged as important
- **Deadlines**: Approaching due dates across all your projects

### How to Use

1. Click the brain icon
2. Select the **Daily Briefing** tab
3. Click **Generate Briefing**
4. Review your personalized summary

### Pro Tips

- Use Daily Briefing first thing in the morning
- Check again after lunch for afternoon priorities
- The briefing updates based on real-time data from your connected services

## Smart Search

Search across Gmail, Calendar, Slack, and your tasks using natural language.

### Example Queries

- "Find emails from John about the marketing proposal"
- "What meetings do I have with the sales team this week?"
- "Show me all tasks related to the website redesign"
- "What did Sarah say about the budget in Slack?"
- "Find calendar events with external clients"

### How to Use

1. Select the **Smart Search** tab
2. Type your question in plain English
3. Press Enter or click Search
4. Browse the results organized by service

### Pro Tips

- Be specific about dates, people, or topics
- Include keywords that would appear in the content
- Combine criteria: "emails from Lisa last week about invoices"

## Email Drafting

Generate professional email responses in seconds.

### How to Use

1. Select the **Email Drafting** tab
2. Describe what you want to communicate:
   - "Thank John for the proposal and ask for a meeting next week"
   - "Politely decline the vendor's offer due to budget constraints"
   - "Follow up on the pending contract with a reminder"
3. Click **Generate Draft**
4. Review and edit the generated email
5. Click **Copy** to paste into Gmail

### Tone Options

The AI adapts to different communication styles:
- **Professional**: Formal business correspondence
- **Friendly**: Warm but professional
- **Brief**: Concise and to-the-point
- **Detailed**: Comprehensive responses

### Pro Tips

- Provide context for better results: "Reply to the budget email from yesterday"
- Mention specific points you want to include
- Always review before sending - the AI helps, you decide

## Task Generation

Convert emails, meeting notes, or any text into actionable tasks.

### How to Use

1. Select the **Task Generation** tab
2. Paste the content (email, notes, etc.)
3. Click **Extract Tasks**
4. Review the suggested tasks
5. Select which tasks to create
6. Choose a project and click **Create**

### What Gets Extracted

- Action items with clear ownership
- Deadlines mentioned in the text
- Follow-up items
- Requests and commitments

### Example Input

> "Hi team, after our meeting I wanted to confirm: Sarah will send the updated proposal by Friday, Ken needs to review the budget numbers, and we should schedule a follow-up call for next Tuesday."

### Generated Tasks

- [ ] Send updated proposal (Assigned: Sarah, Due: Friday)
- [ ] Review budget numbers (Assigned: Ken)
- [ ] Schedule follow-up call for next Tuesday

### Pro Tips

- Paste entire email threads for comprehensive task extraction
- Review extracted tasks - AI may catch items you missed
- Assign to the correct project for better organization

## Meeting Prep

Never walk into a meeting unprepared again.

### How to Use

1. Select the **Meeting Prep** tab
2. Choose an upcoming meeting from the list
3. Click **Prepare**
4. Review the compiled information

### What You'll Get

- **Attendee Profiles**: Who's in the meeting and their recent activity
- **Related Emails**: Recent correspondence with attendees about relevant topics
- **Open Tasks**: Pending items involving attendees or meeting topics
- **Previous Discussions**: Slack messages and past meeting notes
- **Suggested Talking Points**: AI-generated agenda items based on context

### Pro Tips

- Prepare 10-15 minutes before important meetings
- Use the talking points as a starting agenda
- Check for any outstanding action items from previous meetings

## Calendar Optimization

Analyze your schedule to find opportunities for improvement.

### How to Use

1. Select the **Calendar Optimization** tab
2. Choose a time period (this week, next week, this month)
3. Click **Analyze**
4. Review the insights and suggestions

### What You'll Learn

- **Meeting Load**: How much time you spend in meetings
- **Focus Time**: Blocks available for deep work
- **Back-to-Back Patterns**: Consecutive meetings without breaks
- **Overloaded Days**: Days with excessive commitments
- **Optimization Suggestions**: Specific recommendations

### Example Insights

- "Tuesday has 6 hours of meetings with no breaks - consider rescheduling one"
- "You have no focus time blocks this week - try blocking 2 hours on Thursday morning"
- "3 meetings overlap with your usual lunch time"

### Pro Tips

- Review your calendar optimization weekly
- Act on one suggestion at a time
- Share insights with your team to coordinate better

---

## Task Prioritization

Get AI-powered rankings to focus on what matters most.

### How to Use

1. Select the **Task Prioritization** tab
2. Click **Analyze Tasks**
3. Review the prioritized list
4. Focus on the top items first

### Prioritization Factors

The AI considers:
- **Due Dates**: Urgency based on deadlines
- **Dependencies**: Tasks blocking other work
- **Importance**: Impact on goals and projects
- **Effort**: Time required to complete
- **Context**: Related meetings and commitments

### Priority Levels

- **Critical**: Do immediately - overdue or blocking others
- **High**: Complete today if possible
- **Medium**: Schedule for this week
- **Low**: Can wait or delegate

### Pro Tips

- Re-prioritize when new tasks come in
- Focus on completing high-priority items before checking email
- Use this when feeling overwhelmed to regain clarity

## Connecting Services

The AI Assistant works best when your services are connected.

### Recommended Connections

| Service | What It Enables |
|---------|----------------|
| Gmail | Email search, drafting context, message summaries |
| Google Calendar | Meeting prep, schedule optimization, event context |
| Slack | Message search, team discussion context |
| Asana | Task management, project context |

### How to Connect

1. Go to **Settings** > **Connections**
2. Click **Connect** next to each service
3. Authorize access when prompted
4. Return to the AI Assistant

### If a Service Isn't Connected

The AI will show a helpful message explaining what you're missing and how to connect.

## Privacy & Security

### What We Access

- Only data from services you've explicitly connected
- Only when you actively use a feature
- No data is stored beyond your session

### What We Don't Do

- We don't read emails you haven't searched for
- We don't store conversation history
- We don't share data with third parties

### Your Control

- Disconnect any service anytime in Settings
- Clear your session data by closing the panel
- All AI processing happens securely

## Troubleshooting

### "No results found"

- Check that relevant services are connected
- Try broader search terms
- Verify the content exists in your connected accounts

### "Unable to generate"

- Refresh the page and try again
- Check your internet connection
- Ensure services are still connected

### Slow responses

- Large searches may take longer
- Meeting prep with many attendees requires more processing
- Try during off-peak hours for faster results
